Matthew 25 Ministries to send more relief to Ebola victims in Liberia

Matthew 25 Ministries will soon send its second shipment of supplies to help Ebola victims in Liberia. Matthew 25 Ministries will send medical supplies, rice, soy meal packets, gloves, masks, gowns, diapers, soap and other meals, according to Matthew 25 Ministries associate Tim Mettey. Health officials say Liberia has been one of the hardest hit areas during the Ebola outbreak.

Police respond to near drowning involving a 2-year-old at Florence motel

Police said the incident happened at about 7 p.m. at the Super 8 Motel at 7928 Dream St. in Florence. Authorities said a 2-year-old boy was pulled out of the motel pool. Details of the incident are not yet clear.
